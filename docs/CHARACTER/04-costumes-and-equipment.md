# Costumes and Equipment

Book VIII — Character

## Overview

Costumes and equipment define the visual identity and functional role of characters. Clothing, accessories, and tools communicate personality, profession, and progression while reinforcing the overall art direction of the 3D portfolio.

## Costume Design Principles

- Visual consistency
- Functional design
- Readable silhouettes
- Color harmony
- Material diversity

## Equipment Categories

- Clothing
- Accessories
- Tools
- Gadgets
- Wearable technology
- Collectible items

## Design Workflow

1. Concept sketches
2. Material selection
3. Color exploration
4. 3D modeling
5. Texture creation
6. Rigging and attachment
7. In-engine validation

## Best Practices

- Match costumes to character roles.
- Reuse modular assets where possible.
- Ensure compatibility with animations.
- Optimize geometry and textures.
- Document costume variants and equipment sets.

Well-designed costumes and equipment enhance storytelling, strengthen character identity, and contribute to an immersive user experience.
