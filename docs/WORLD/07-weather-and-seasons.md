# Weather & Seasons

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define dynamic environmental conditions that enrich the cinematic experience without distracting from the portfolio.

## WEATHER-001 Supported Seasons
- Spring (default): Cherry blossoms, fresh greenery.
- Summer: Lush vegetation, bright skies.
- Autumn: Red and golden foliage.
- Winter: Light snow, frosted rooftops, bare trees.

## WEATHER-002 Weather States
- Clear
- Light Rain
- Misty Morning
- Light Snow (winter only)

## WEATHER-003 Visual Rules
- Weather transitions are smooth.
- Portfolio content remains readable.
- Particle effects are subtle.
- Reflections respond to rain.

## WEATHER-004 Audio
- Birds in spring.
- Cicadas in summer.
- Wind through leaves in autumn.
- Soft snowfall ambience in winter.
- Rain ambience during showers.

## WEATHER-005 Performance
- Disable expensive particles on lower-end devices.
- Adaptive quality settings.

## Acceptance Criteria
Weather and seasons should enhance immersion and reinforce the feeling of a living world while preserving smooth performance and usability.
