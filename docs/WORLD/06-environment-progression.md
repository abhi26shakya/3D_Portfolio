# Environment Progression

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define how the world evolves as the visitor scrolls. The transformation should mirror Abhishek's personal growth while preserving the feeling of one continuous place.

## ENV-001 Progression Philosophy
The environment matures alongside the protagonist. Changes should be gradual, never abrupt.

## Stage 1 – Childhood
- Cozy village homes
- Dirt paths
- Rice fields
- Wooden fences
- Morning light

## Stage 2 – Learning
- School buildings
- Library
- More structured roads
- Study areas

## Stage 3 – Engineering
- Workshop
- Electronics benches
- Drone practice field
- Modern equipment integrated into the rural setting

## Stage 4 – Research
- Contemporary research campus
- Glass architecture
- AI-inspired installations
- Collaboration spaces

## Stage 5 – Future
- Mountain shrine
- Open road
- Expansive horizon
- Symbol of lifelong learning

## Transition Rules
- Blend vegetation, architecture and lighting naturally.
- Preserve visual continuity of the main road.
- Character growth from boy to adult should synchronize with environmental evolution.

## Acceptance Criteria
Visitors should feel they have travelled through time and personal development without ever leaving the same living world.
