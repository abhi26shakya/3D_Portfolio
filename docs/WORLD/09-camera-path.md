# Camera Path

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define how the virtual camera guides the visitor through the portfolio. The camera should feel cinematic, comfortable and always support the story.

## CAM-001 Camera Style
- Third-person follow camera.
- Smooth spring interpolation.
- Slight over-the-shoulder perspective.

## CAM-002 Scroll Behavior
- Vertical page scrolling moves the character.
- Camera follows with easing.
- Never snap between viewpoints.

## CAM-003 Landmark Framing
- Briefly widen the field of view before each major landmark.
- Keep the character and destination visible together.

## CAM-004 Hero Shots
- Childhood Home: warm medium shot.
- Research Campus: wider establishing shot.
- Phone Booth: intimate close framing.
- Mountain Shrine: cinematic pullback revealing the entire journey.

## CAM-005 Accessibility
- Respect reduced-motion preferences.
- Limit sudden rotation.
- Keep horizon stable.

## Acceptance Criteria
Visitors should feel guided naturally through the environment while maintaining clear visibility of portfolio content and surroundings.
