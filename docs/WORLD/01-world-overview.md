# World Overview

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define the overall structure of the interactive world. The portfolio exists within one seamless Japanese-inspired village that evolves as the visitor scrolls. There are no disconnected pages—only one continuous journey.

## WORLD-001 Vision
The world represents Abhishek's personal and professional growth, beginning in a peaceful rural setting and gradually expanding into environments that reflect education, engineering, research and future ambitions.

## WORLD-002 Journey Route
1. Village Entrance (Intro)
2. Childhood Home (About)
3. School & Library (Education)
4. Electronics Workshop (Skills)
5. Drone Field & Robotics Area (Projects)
6. Research Campus (Research)
7. Collaboration Square (Experience)
8. Cherry Blossom Avenue (Achievements)
9. Red Phone Booth (Contact)
10. Mountain Shrine (Journey Continues)

## WORLD-003 Design Rules
- One continuous road.
- No loading screens.
- No teleportation.
- Smooth environmental transitions.
- Landmarks visible from a distance.

## WORLD-004 Character Progression
The protagonist gradually grows from a curious village boy into a confident engineer and researcher. The transformation should be subtle and synchronized with story milestones.

## Acceptance Criteria
Visitors should perceive the entire portfolio as one cohesive world where every location naturally introduces the next chapter of the journey.
