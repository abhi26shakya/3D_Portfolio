# NPC System

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
NPCs (Non-Player Characters) make the village feel alive while subtly guiding visitors through the portfolio. They are supporting characters, never the focus.

## NPC-001 Design Principles
- Calm and natural behavior.
- No dialogue bubbles unless interacted with.
- Low-frequency ambient animations.
- Never obstruct the main path.

## NPC Categories
1. Parents (Childhood Home)
2. Children (Village School)
3. Teachers & Librarians
4. Farmers (Rice Fields)
5. Craftsmen (Workshop)
6. Engineers (Research Campus)
7. Mentors & Collaborators (Collaboration Square)
8. Villagers & Tourists (Ambient)

## NPC Behaviors
- Walking
- Reading
- Gardening
- Fishing
- Working
- Conversing
- Observing scenery

## Interaction Rules
- Subtle greetings when nearby.
- Optional interactions reveal small story details.
- Important NPCs gently direct attention toward the next landmark.

## Performance Constraints
- Maximum 20 active NPCs.
- Use animation instancing where possible.
- Reduce NPC density on lower-end devices.

## Acceptance Criteria
NPCs should enhance immersion without distracting from the portfolio or reducing rendering performance.
