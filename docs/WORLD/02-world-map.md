# World Map

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define the spatial layout of the portfolio world. The visitor should travel through a single continuous route where each location represents a chapter of Abhishek's journey.

## WORLDMAP-001 Overall Layout
The world follows a gently winding road through a fictional Japanese-inspired valley.

Route:
Entrance Gate → Childhood Home → Village School → Library → Electronics Workshop → Drone Field → Research Campus → Collaboration Square → Cherry Blossom Avenue → Red Phone Booth → Mountain Shrine.

## WORLDMAP-002 Navigation
- Scrolling advances the character.
- Camera follows automatically.
- No minimap.
- No teleportation.

## WORLDMAP-003 Landmarks
Each landmark must be visible before the visitor reaches it to encourage curiosity.

## WORLDMAP-004 Portfolio Mapping
- About → Childhood Home
- Education → School & Library
- Skills → Workshop
- Projects → Drone Field
- Research → Research Campus
- Experience → Collaboration Square
- Achievements → Cherry Blossom Avenue
- Contact → Phone Booth

## WORLDMAP-005 Journey Ending
Beyond the shrine, the road continues into distant mountains, symbolizing lifelong learning and future ambitions.

## Acceptance Criteria
The visitor should always understand where they are within the journey without requiring a traditional navigation menu.
