# Landmarks

# BOOK IV — WORLD

## Purpose
Define the iconic locations that structure the visitor's journey. Each landmark should introduce a portfolio chapter through environmental storytelling before any UI appears.

## LAND-001 Entrance Gate
Purpose: Welcome visitors.
Scene: Traditional wooden torii-inspired gateway with your name subtly engraved.
Portfolio: Introduction.

## LAND-002 Childhood Home
Purpose: About section.
Elements: Family home, books, bicycle, childhood memories.

## LAND-003 Village School
Purpose: Education.
Elements: Classroom, library, notebooks, graduation path.

## LAND-004 Electronics Workshop
Purpose: Skills.
Elements: Circuit boards, oscilloscopes, soldering bench, embedded systems.

## LAND-005 Drone Field
Purpose: Projects.
Elements: Flying drones, prototypes, interactive project displays.

## LAND-006 Research Campus
Purpose: Research.
Elements: Modern glass building, AI visualization, publications, research posters.

## LAND-007 Collaboration Square
Purpose: Experience.
Elements: Mentors, teammates, internships, open-source contributions.

## LAND-008 Cherry Blossom Avenue
Purpose: Achievements.
Elements: Awards, milestones, conference banners beneath blooming trees.

## LAND-009 Red Phone Booth
Purpose: Contact.
Elements: Contact links, CV download, GitHub, LinkedIn and email revealed through interaction.

## LAND-010 Mountain Shrine
Purpose: Ending.
Message: 'The Journey Continues.' The road extends beyond the visible world toward future ambitions.

## Acceptance Criteria
Each landmark should be visually distinctive, emotionally memorable and immediately associated with its corresponding portfolio section.
