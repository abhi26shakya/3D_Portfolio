# Success Metrics

## Purpose
Define measurable outcomes for evaluating whether the portfolio achieves its objectives.

## Product Goals
- Create a memorable first impression.
- Clearly communicate Abhishek's journey.
- Increase opportunities for research, internships, and collaboration.

## Experience KPIs
### Engagement
- Average visit duration greater than 3 minutes.
- Visitors explore most major sections.

### Storytelling
- Visitors understand the progression from village boy to engineer.
- Visitors can identify key milestones without reading excessive text.

### Professional Impact
- Increased recruiter and professor outreach.
- Resume downloads.
- Contact form submissions.
- GitHub and LinkedIn visits.

## Technical KPIs
- Lighthouse Performance: 90+
- Accessibility: 95+
- Best Practices: 95+
- SEO: 95+
- Stable 60 FPS on modern desktops.

## Visual Quality Goals
- Consistent cinematic presentation.
- Smooth scrolling.
- Responsive layout.
- No distracting animations.

## Review Checklist
The project should be reviewed after each milestone to ensure both technical quality and storytelling quality continue to improve.
