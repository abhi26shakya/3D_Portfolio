# User Personas

## Purpose
This document defines the primary audiences for the portfolio and the information each audience expects to discover.

---

# Persona 1 — Research Professor

## Goal
Evaluate research potential, technical depth, curiosity, and ability to contribute to academic work.

## Needs
- Research interests
- Publications
- Technical background
- Evidence of independent learning
- Clear contact information

## Portfolio Priorities
Research → Experience → Projects → Resume → Contact

---

# Persona 2 — Technical Recruiter

## Goal
Determine whether Abhishek is a strong engineering candidate.

## Needs
- Skills
- Experience
- Projects
- Resume
- GitHub
- LinkedIn

## Portfolio Priorities
Experience → Projects → Skills → Resume

---

# Persona 3 — Startup Founder

## Goal
Find someone capable of building innovative products.

## Needs
- Initiative
- Product thinking
- Technical versatility
- Collaboration

## Portfolio Priorities
Projects → Story → Skills → Contact

---

# Persona 4 — Fellow Engineer or Student

## Goal
Learn from the journey and explore technical work.

## Needs
- Source code
- Technical write-ups
- Learning resources
- Project details

---

# Design Implications
Every section of the portfolio should answer a real question that one of these personas is likely to ask. Decorative elements should never obscure access to important information.

## Success Criteria
A visitor from each persona should be able to locate the information most relevant to them within a few minutes while still experiencing the overall story.
