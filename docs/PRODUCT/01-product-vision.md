# Product Vision Document (PVD)

**Project:** The Journey of Abhishek

## Purpose
Create a cinematic, interactive 3D portfolio website that tells the story of Abhishek Kumar Shakya's journey from a curious village boy to an AI researcher and engineer.

## Product Definition
This is a professional portfolio website, not a game. The visitor experiences the journey by scrolling through a continuous Japanese-inspired village environment.

## Primary Goals
- Showcase research, projects, experience, and skills.
- Leave a memorable impression on professors, recruiters, and collaborators.
- Demonstrate technical excellence in modern web engineering.

## Core Principles
1. Story first.
2. Portfolio clarity over visual spectacle.
3. Cinematic, not game-like.
4. Performance and accessibility are mandatory.
5. Modular, production-ready architecture.

## Success Criteria
A visitor should finish the experience understanding:
- Who Abhishek is.
- What he has built.
- What he researches.
- Why they should collaborate with him.
