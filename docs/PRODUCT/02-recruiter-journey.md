# Recruiter Journey Map

## Objective
Design every stage of the portfolio around the questions a professor, recruiter, or collaborator is likely to ask.

## Visitor Timeline

### 0–30 seconds
Goal: Create curiosity.
- A peaceful Japanese-inspired village.
- A young boy begins walking.
- Minimal UI.
- Visitor understands this is a portfolio, not a game.

### 30–90 seconds
Goal: Build trust.
- Reveal Abhishek's identity naturally.
- Introduce motivation and background.
- Establish professionalism through design quality.

### 90–180 seconds
Goal: Demonstrate capability.
- Education, research and experience appear through the environment.
- Projects become interactive exhibits.
- Skills are communicated visually with supporting text.

### Final Minute
Goal: Encourage collaboration.
- Contact phone booth becomes the emotional conclusion.
- Resume download and contact information are easily accessible.
- Visitor leaves with a clear understanding of who Abhishek is and why collaboration makes sense.

## Success Criteria
- The visitor remembers the person before the technology.
- Navigation is effortless.
- Every section answers a real hiring or collaboration question.
- The experience motivates further conversation.
