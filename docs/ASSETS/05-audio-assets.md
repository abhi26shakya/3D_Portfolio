# Audio Assets

Book VI — Assets

## Overview

Audio assets bring interactive experiences to life through music, sound effects, ambient sounds, and voice recordings. A well-managed audio pipeline improves immersion while maintaining performance and consistency.

## Types of Audio Assets

- Background Music (BGM)
- Sound Effects (SFX)
- Ambient Audio
- Voice Overs
- UI Sounds
- Spatial Audio

## Common Formats

- WAV
- MP3
- OGG Vorbis
- FLAC
- AAC

## Best Practices

- Use lossless formats during production.
- Compress assets appropriately for deployment.
- Normalize audio levels.
- Organize assets by category.
- Optimize streaming versus preloading based on asset size.

High-quality audio assets enhance storytelling, usability, and player engagement.
