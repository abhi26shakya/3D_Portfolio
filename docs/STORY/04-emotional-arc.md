# Emotional Arc

## Purpose
This document defines the intended emotional progression of every visitor. Every scene, animation, sound and interaction should reinforce this journey.

| Stage | Scene | Emotion | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| EA-001 | Opening Village | Curiosity | Encourage exploration |
| EA-002 | Childhood | Warmth | Build a personal connection |
| EA-003 | Education | Determination | Show discipline and growth |
| EA-004 | Engineering | Excitement | Demonstrate technical ability |
| EA-005 | Research | Wonder | Inspire confidence in research potential |
| EA-006 | Experience | Trust | Showcase professionalism and collaboration |
| EA-007 | Contact | Connection | Invite meaningful conversation |
| EA-008 | Epilogue | Inspiration | Leave a lasting memory |

## Design Requirements
- Music, lighting and animation should gradually evolve with the story.
- Day should transition naturally into evening.
- The character's transformation from boy to man should feel subtle and believable.
- Emotional changes should be driven by environmental storytelling rather than abrupt UI changes.

## Acceptance Criteria
A first-time visitor should finish the experience feeling they have accompanied a real journey rather than navigated a traditional portfolio website.
